The New Era of Entrepreneurship

Join us for the National Entrepreneurship Summit (NES) at Chitkara University, Punjab! Our one-day event brings together entrepreneurs from across the country to learn, connect, and share experiences. With 11 expert speakers covering topics from ideation to scaling, attendees will have the opportunity to gain valuable insights, network, and explore new business opportunities.

Meet 11 top entrepreneurs
in a unique 1-day event.

Valuable Insight

Learn from the experts at NES! Gain valuable insights from our lineup of expert speakers covering various domains, and take away practical strategies to apply to your own business.

National Audience

NES is a national event for entrepreneurs, investors, and industry experts from diverse backgrounds. Join us to network, exchange ideas, and explore new business opportunities with like-minded individuals from across the country.

Great Community

Our event provides a platform to connect, collaborate and explore new business opportunities with like-minded individuals. Be a part of a supportive and dynamic community of entrepreneurs by attending NES.
